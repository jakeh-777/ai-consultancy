The Problem

AI sounds great in theory. But when it comes to actually using it, most teams get stuck at the same point: where do we even start?

The opportunity is too broad. The tools are too general. And the gap between "AI could help with that" and actually solving the problem feels massive.

The real questions are specific: What's taking too long? What's not working? What does your team keep failing to hit? What tools do you already use that don't give you what you need?

These questions exist in every business. AI genuinely can help answer them. But only if someone connects the technology to the actual problem.
